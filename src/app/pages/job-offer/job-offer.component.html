{{'job_offer_requirements'|translate}}

            1. Assist in developing comprehensive RFPs for Design-Build and contract document deliverables, and
            negotiate scope-of-work, design briefs, deliverables, and price with the A/E.

            2. Assist Project Managers in internal and external design and cost issues.

            3. Manage distribution and production of project workload within the team.

            4. Participate in departmental strategic planning.

            5. Lead and manage design and technical functions associated with the Capital program.

            6. To ensure effective communication and negotiation between all client stakeholders.

            7. To make recommendations on design and technical issues.

            8. To encourage and promote the continuous improvement of working methods including collaborative
            working, partnering, and procurement and project management.

            9. Candidate should be familiar with all Saudi codes and standards, the International Building Code
            (IBC), OmniClass, UniFormat and MasterFormat Specification classifications, and be familiar with
            standards as promoted by both the Royal Institute of British Architects (RIBA) and American institute of
            Architects (AIA). Candidate must be familiar with General Design Criteria and Technical Guidelines,
            standard drawings and standard guide specifications.

            10. Assists in the oversight and review as necessary of A/E contractor design submittals, specifications,
            calculations, as initially reviewed by the PMC, and coordinate work of other disciplines and the
            engineering department of the PMO.

            11. Review the preparation of technical specifications and drawings as needed for bid packages related to
            the development of various sports venues, etc. for design and construction projects.

            12. Assists in the review of vendor drawings and shop drawings and coordinates with the PMO Engineering
            Manager the distribution of these reviews among the various disciplines of the PMO Engineering
            Department.

            13. Performs various engineering calculations, as necessary and/or distributes these tasks to discipline
            engineers on staff with the PMO.

            14. Performs PMO oversight guidance and potential field inspection and field support, as necessary.
